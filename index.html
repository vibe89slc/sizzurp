This Ain't Your Average Soda Shop

Born in the heart of downtown Salt Lake City, Sizzurp Soda Lab is where flavor goes to get weird — in the best way possible.

We took the dirty soda and gave it a full makeover. Named after hip-hop's most notorious purple potion, we keep the energy high and the drinks even higher. From classic filthy combos to energy-fueled uppers and creamy, decadent downers — every cup is a whole mood.

Whether you're here for the bold names, the bolder flavors, or just a damn good drink — welcome to the lab. Dose responsibly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sizzurp Soda Lab?

Sizzurp Soda Lab is a custom dirty soda shop in downtown Salt Lake City at 150 S State St. We serve bold, creative soda combinations in three categories: Filthy (classic soda + syrup), Uppers (energy-based with Monster and Red Bull), and Downers (soda + fruit purées + cream).

What sizes do you offer?

We offer four sizes: Microdose (16 oz), Dose (24 oz), Double Dose (32 oz), and Overdose (44 oz). Same fun names — just pick how deep you’re going. All drinks are available in every size.

Can Sizzurp Soda Lab cater my event?

Absolutely. We bring the lab to your event — weddings, corporate events, parties, you name it. We set up with a curated selection of our signature flavors and all four dose sizes. Hit us up on Instagram @sizzurpsodalab to book.

How can I order online?

Order directly through Toast for pickup, or get delivery through DoorDash. Or just come see us at 150 S State St.

What's a dirty soda?

A dirty soda is a fountain soda mixed with flavored syrups, fruit purées, cream, and other add-ins to create unique, custom flavor combinations. Think of it as a soda cocktail — minus the alcohol, plus all the personality. Sizzurp takes the concept and cranks it way up.
